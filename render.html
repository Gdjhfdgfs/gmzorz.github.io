  • Framerate (FPS): Same as source

  • Preset: Placebo*
  • Tune: none
  • Profile: high

    *The speed preset has the biggest influence on the quality
      Placebo = Best quality & Extremely slow rendering
      Ultrafast = Low Quality & Extremely fast rendering
      (Speed depends on computer specs)

    **2-pass encoding = double rendering, better quality, optional in my opinion. if you have all the time in the world i suggest using this
